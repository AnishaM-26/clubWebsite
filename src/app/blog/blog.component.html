Can we Drink Sea Water?

We well know the fact that 70 per cent of the earth is covered with water. But only 2 to 3 per cent of it is freshwater. And the rest is saline and ocean-based. The freshwater quantity is always constant and it only recycled by nature (atmosphere ). Even the two-third of freshwater is not available in liquid form — It’s in frozen form. Groundwater is the major source of drinking water, it provides 25 to 40 per cent of earth’s drinking water.
